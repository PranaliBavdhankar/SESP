Dear Students and Parents,

Solapur Education Society’s Polytechnic welcomes you to become a member of the family with rich culture, built on strong pillars of human values with a vision of molding the entrants into responsible engineers, who will not only help themselves but also the society to become a part of the changing world and create impressions of their existence. We at S. E. S. Polytechnic are committed to provide quality education so as to impart skills required for becoming industry ready –both for grabbing jobs and also becoming entrepreneurs.Since the inception, our students have proved their abilities and contributed to the field of engineering. Many of our alumni are at topmost positions in large scale industries. Market Leaders such as Tata Motors, Bajaj, Mahindra & Mahindra, L&T, Precision Camshafts, Kirloskar Ferrous and many more conduct pool recruitment drives in our campus. We look forward to a large number of students joining S. E. S. Polytechnic and be a part of the skilling and getting empowered. Wishing and praying a great success ahead.
